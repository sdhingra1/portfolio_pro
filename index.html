What It Does

One Tool That Brings Your Entire Financial Life Into Focus

Most retirees hold accounts at two, three, even five different institutions. Seeing the complete picture — across all accounts, all asset classes, all income streams — has never been easy. Until now.

📊

See Your True Asset Allocation

Upload statements from all your accounts and see your real allocation across stocks, bonds, and cash — including what's inside balanced funds and ETFs.

  • Automatic look-through for mixed funds (JEPI, FBKFX, AOM)
  • GICS sector breakdown for individual stocks
  • Side-by-side comparison against your target allocation
  • Rebalancing trade recommendations
💵

Understand Your Income & Tax Efficiency

Know exactly how much income your portfolio generates each year — and how much of it flows to taxes versus your pocket.

  • Income classified as tax-free, tax-deferred, or taxable
  • Tax-Equivalent Yield calculations for your exact bracket
  • State-specific muni bond analysis for all 50 states
  • Asset location diagnostics — catch costly placement errors
🎯

Plan Your Retirement Income Strategy

Set income targets for each account type and receive a personalized, actionable plan with specific instruments, TEY calculations, and a full buy/sell action table.

  • 14 granular income strategies across all account types
  • BDC, CLO, covered-call, and muni bond recommendations
  • Specific buy/sell action plan with estimated income gains
  • Roth conversion analysis and asset location fixes

Balanced funds, target-date funds, and covered-call ETFs are automatically "looked through" to their underlying stock/bond/cash split — giving you a true asset allocation, not a misleading one.

State-Specific Tax Intelligence

How To Use It

From Statements to Strategy in Four Steps

No financial software to install. No account linking. Just your brokerage statements and a few minutes.

1
📥

Download Your Statements

Log in to each brokerage and download your positions as a CSV file. Most brokerages make this very easy.

Fidelity Schwab Vanguard Chase Merrill
2
📤

Upload Into PortfolioPro

Drag and drop your files — or click to browse. The app automatically detects your brokerage format and parses every position, bond, and fund.

3
✏️

Name & Classify Accounts

Give each account a name (e.g., "Fidelity Roth IRA") and confirm its tax treatment — taxable, tax-deferred, or tax-free. Takes under a minute.

4
🔓

Set a PIN & Explore

Set a private PIN to encrypt your data in the browser. Then explore your allocation, income, and income strategy — your data persists securely between sessions.

Your Privacy

Your Financial Data Belongs to You. Only You.

We understand the sensitivity of financial information. PortfolioPro was architected from the ground up with one principle: your data should never leave your control.

💻

Runs Entirely In Your Browser

There is no server. No database. No cloud storage. Every calculation happens on your own computer using only your browser's built-in capabilities. Your files are never uploaded anywhere.

🔐

PIN-Protected Encryption

If you choose to save your session, data is encrypted using AES-256-GCM — the same standard used by financial institutions. Your PIN is never stored. Without it, the saved data is computationally unreadable.

🚪

You Are in Complete Control

Lock your session anytime to wipe data from memory. Download an encrypted backup. Or clear everything with one click. No account deletion forms. No support tickets. Your data, your rules.
